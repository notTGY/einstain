Why I started this

So, Idea for this website came to me from a dialog. Here is what I was told. Check this out, this is incredible:

We have looked up your previous websites and haven't seen any fancy CSS going there. We think you don't have talent in styling a webpage.

To be honest, I was so shocked... I didn't answer anything, so buzzed off I was. Later this perfect answer came to my head:

I can do everything.

Beutiful and genious, how simple it is. At least I'm being jackass not thinking so fast to "return message to the sender".

Why my projects seem to lack CSS

From the start of my learning path till now I had this clear idea of what I want my works to look like. And design was not the main goal. Basically, I firstly want to make something that works and actually does meaningful things, not postcard sites.

That's why I spent most of my time coding some amazing ideas into life. I rushed from project to project in order to complete more templates for myself to find an idea, that would hook me the most. To implement something actually useful.
