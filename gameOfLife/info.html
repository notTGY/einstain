This is Game Of Life

This is pretty thick - Turing complete cellular automaton!

For real! This is just limited area of cells, which have two states: alive and dead. When you play this "Game" you can (by left-clicking) make any cell change it`s state. By right-clicking you will start game (also pause it once started) and you will see some things happening according to the rules.

Rules are:

  • If cell has more than 3 alive neighbours it becomes dead
  • If cell has less than 2 alive neighbours it becomes dead
  • If cell has exactly 3 alive neighbours it becomes alive

Pretty straight-forward, right? But you can create things with endless possibilities inside of this.

For getting around you can use WASD or Arrows. To zoom in and out use + and -. To speed up time or slow it down use > or <.
